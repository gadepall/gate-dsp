
The energy of the signal $f(t)$ is given as.
\begin{align}
    E = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} |f\brak{t}|^2 dt
\end{align}
The energy of signal $f(2t)$,
\begin{align}
    E' = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} |f\brak{2t}|^2 dt
\end{align}
Putting $u = 2t$,
\begin{align}
    du = 2dt\\
    E' = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} |f\brak{u}|^2 \frac{du}{2} = \frac{E}{2}
\end{align}
